About Me

Hello! I'm Nayem Uddin, a dedicated Software Developer and Tech Enthusiast based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I have professional experience as a Software Developer at Gigabit Ltd (January 2024-Ongoing) where I collaborate with backend and frontend teams, meet clients, and participate in the full development lifecycle from planning to deployment. I also worked as a Laravel Developer at Wetopia Global (October 2023 - December 2023), focusing on full-stack web development with Laravel and Vue.js and Three-Tier Architecture.

My journey in software development began with a fascination for how systems work behind the scenes, leading me to specialize in creating efficient and scalable solutions. I thrive on solving complex problems and am passionate about writing clean, maintainable code. My expertise lies in building high-performance APIs, managing databases, and integrating various services to create seamless digital experiences. I am always eager to learn new technologies and improve my craft.

I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from City University, Dhaka (March 2019-December 2023). I actively participate in competitive programming, having solved over 500 problems on various online judges like HackerRank and LeetCode, and participated in over 90 individual programming contests.
